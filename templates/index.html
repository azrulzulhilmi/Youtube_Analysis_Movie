📈 Polarity Score

This score ranges from -1.0 to +1.0. A higher value (closer to 1) means the transcript uses highly positive and happy language. A lower value (closer to -1) indicates sad, negative, or angry language. A score around 0 indicates a neutral tone.

🔄 Sentiment Context

By looking at the Polarity and Emotion breakdown together, we can understand the overall mood. If negative words are high alongside "Sadness" or "Anger", it usually reflects drama-like emotional lows.
